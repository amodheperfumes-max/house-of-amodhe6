Café Ambré

Café Ambré is an extrait de parfum built around dark roasted coffee and warm amber.

The opening is dry, bitter, and restrained, avoiding sweetness or brightness.

As it settles, amber softens the coffee, creating a quiet warmth that stays close to the skin.

Craft and Time

Time is treated as a material, never a constraint.

Each composition is allowed to rest, mature, and settle naturally.

Nothing is rushed. Nothing is forced.

Edition Statement

Café Ambré is released once each month.

Every edition is limited to twenty bottles only.

When sold out, production stops until the next cycle.

Why We Limit Production

Scale reduces attention. Speed reduces care.

Limitation allows every decision to remain personal.

Quality is protected by staying small.

Quiet Questions

Is Café Ambré gendered? No. It is composed for skin, not identity.

Longevity? As an extrait, it evolves slowly and lasts for hours.

Availability? Released once per month in limited quantity.

The House of Amodhe

A quiet alternative to modern perfumery.

Designed for intimacy, depth, and restraint.

This is slow perfumery. This is Amodhe.
